### Decks 2.5.4 — faster Anki imports, with audio and images that just work

Importing a large Anki deck used to crawl, and some cards showed raw `![[sound.mp3]]` text instead of playing anything. Decks 2.5.4 makes `.apkg` import dramatically faster and gets media rendering the way it should.

#### **Media copies in seconds, not minutes**

Decks with thousands of images and audio clips now import their media far faster — a deck whose media took minutes to copy finishes in seconds.

#### **Audio and video play in review**

Cards imported from Anki now show their images *and* play their audio (and video) during review. Previously, cards built from Anki's styled templates displayed the raw `![[audio.mp3]]` embed text instead of a player — now they render a proper audio/video control, just like images already did.

#### **A heads-up on very large imports**

When you import a deck with thousands of media files, Decks now lets you know Obsidian is indexing them in the background — so a brief moment before every image and sound appears is expected, not a glitch.

> Upgrade-safe: nothing in your vault changes, and re-importing a deck refreshes its media without creating duplicates.

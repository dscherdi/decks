### Changes

#### **Deck name column always visible in narrow panels**

In languages with long translations for the "New" and "Due" column headers (e.g. Russian "К повторению"), the deck name column was being squeezed out of view on narrow sidebars, making it impossible to click on a deck.

The stat column headers are now capped in width and truncate with an ellipsis when the translation is too long. On narrow viewports the column gap is also reduced. The deck name column is always visible and clickable regardless of language or panel width.

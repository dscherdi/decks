### Decks 2.8.1 — scratchpad

This release adds a scratchpad to review — a surface for working out an answer by drawing or typing before you reveal the card.

#### **Draw or type while reviewing**

A pen button sits with the card's other controls, just before the card-actions menu, in normal review, browse, and cram. It opens a scratchpad over the card with a surface you can draw on by finger, mouse, or pen, and a text box for typed notes. The toolbar has pen, eraser, text, undo, clear, and close. The rating and reveal buttons stay in place below the scratchpad.

#### **Per card, not saved**

The scratchpad is scoped to the current card and isn't stored. Its drawing and text clear when you move to the next card or close the session.

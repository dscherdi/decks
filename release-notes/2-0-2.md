### Decks 2.0.2 — better AI generation

A follow-up to 2.0 focused on the AI generator: keep generating without losing your work, pick models from a list, open it in a tab, plus a round of readability fixes. AI features stay **off until you add your own provider key** in **Settings → AI**.

#### **Keep generating after saving**

The prompt box no longer disappears once you save. Generate a batch, save it, and keep going — newly generated cards are added to the list instead of replacing it, and already-saved cards are marked and excluded from the next save so you never write duplicates. After the first save the generator switches to **append** mode and auto-selects the deck you just created, so follow-up batches land in the same deck.

#### **Use your cards as context**

A new toggle in the prompt box (next to the attach buttons) feeds the cards generated so far back to the model, so a follow-up round avoids repeating them. Your typed prompt always comes last, so it stays in charge.

#### **Pick a model from a list**

The **Model** field is now a dropdown of current models per provider (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini), with a **Custom…** option for any model id not listed. Defaults were refreshed to current-generation models.

#### **Open the generator in a tab**

A new **AI generator display mode** setting opens the generator in a workspace tab instead of a modal — draggable to sidebars, bottom panels, or separate windows. It collapses to a single pane when the tab is narrow, just like the modal does on small screens.

#### **Fixes & polish**

- The **Generate with AI** button is now disabled while AI features are off, and updates live when you toggle the setting.
- **Enable AI features** moved to the top of the AI settings block.
- Error messages in the generator are now readable (previously a blank red bar) and long provider errors scroll instead of filling the panel.
- Selecting a model no longer clears the rest of the settings tab.
- The **Save** button in profile and deck-config dialogs is legible again across themes.
- Fixed a duplicated label in the Simplified Chinese translation.

> Upgrade-safe: nothing in your vault changes.

### Decks 2.8.4 — flat view and a tighter deck list

This release adds a tree/flat toggle to the Decks panel and makes the whole list more compact.

#### **Switch between tree and flat**

A small toggle in the filter bar switches the deck list between the nested tree and a flat list. In flat view every file deck, tag, and custom deck is listed directly under its Files / Tags / Custom section, with no folder or sub-tag nesting; the Pinned block still sits on top in both. Your choice is remembered and synced across devices.

#### **More compact layout**

Rows are tighter and sized to their content, so more decks fit on screen. The column header and the toolbar take less vertical space, and the toolbar and filter-bar icon buttons are square.

### Decks 2.5.2 — open and preview linked notes while reviewing

A lot of cards point to other notes instead of repeating their content. Now those links behave during review exactly like they do everywhere else in Obsidian.

#### **Internal links come alive in the review window**

If a card's front or back links to another note — `[[Some Note#Heading]]` or `[text](../folder/note.md#heading)` — you can now:

- **Hold Ctrl/Cmd and hover** a link to see the linked note in a preview popover, without leaving your review.
- **Click** a link to open that note and jump straight to the linked heading or block (Ctrl/Cmd-click opens it in a new tab).

You can adjust the hover preview (including whether it needs the Ctrl/Cmd key) under **Settings → Core plugins → Page preview**, where Decks now appears alongside your other sources.

> Upgrade-safe: nothing in your vault changes.

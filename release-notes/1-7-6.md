### Changes

**Undo last review**
- New undo button (top-left of the card) appears as soon as you've rated at least one card in the current session — click it to roll back your most recent rating
- Cmd/Ctrl+Z works as a keyboard shortcut for the same action
- The card's full FSRS state (stability, difficulty, due date, interval, repetitions, lapses) is restored exactly to its pre-review values, so an accidental "Easy" tap doesn't push the card weeks or months out
- The session progress counter rewinds correctly, including the case where you rated the same card twice (only the latest review is undone)
- Undo can be used repeatedly to walk all the way back through the current session — keep pressing it to unwind every rating you've made since opening the modal
- Closing the review modal ends the session, so undo is always scoped to your current sitting and won't affect history from earlier sessions

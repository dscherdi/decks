### Decks 2.5.3 — customizable review shortcuts

Reviewing feels better when your fingers barely move. Decks 2.5.3 lets you remap the five review keys to whatever layout suits you.

#### **Set your own review keys**

The four rating keys (Again / Hard / Good / Easy) and the reveal key are now fully customizable — go home-row with `asdf` + Space, right-hand with `jkl;` + Space, Vim-style with `hjkl`, or anything else you like.

Open **Settings → Keyboard shortcuts**, click the key next to an action, and press the key you want to use. The shortcut hints shown during review update to match, and **Reset to defaults** restores `1-4` + Space at any time.

The **Enable keyboard shortcuts** switch now turns the whole set (ratings, reveal, and the bury/suspend/reset keys) on or off together.

> Upgrade-safe: your existing `1-4` / Space shortcuts keep working until you change them.
